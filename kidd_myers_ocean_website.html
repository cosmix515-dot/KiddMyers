About Me

KiddMyers began his music journey in late 2024, first experimenting with a Gorilla Tag inspired track. The song’s title is now unknown, as his original channel, CosmicGT, has since been deleted. In January 2025, KiddMyers released “Five Dolla’s Ft. CxRxT,” marking his first real step into the music scene. The track was initially uploaded to an account called CosmixMusic before being reuploaded to a newer channel. Known for its explicit and comedic tone, KiddMyers even clarified at the end of the song that it was meant as a troll. After this release, he took a step back from music for most of 2025. He returned on November 15th, 2025, with “Five Dolla’s Ft. CxRxT (Part 2)” on his second channel, More Cosmix. Continuing the series, he dropped “Five Dolla’s Ft. CxRxT (Part 3)” on January 30th, 2026, around the anniversary of the original track. Although initially intended to be the final installment, KiddMyers later decided to continue the series, finding the creative process enjoyable alongside Stojinator. In early 2026, KiddMyers made the decision to take music more seriously, releasing his official debut track, “Pop It.” Looking ahead, he’s expressed strong excitement for future releases, calling this moment “the start of something big.”
